RC-Gamepad-Dongle

Complete RC-to-USB joystick solution that converts RC receiver signals (IBUS, PPM, SBUS, CRSF, DSMX, DSM2, FPORT) into standard USB HID joystick inputs. Features Arduino-based firmware with a desktop configurator app for easy setup. Supports 6 axes, 32 buttons, and 2 hat switches - perfect for flight simulators, robot control, and custom gaming setups.
